About Our Technology

Our application utilizes a VGG16 Convolutional Neural Network (CNN) for the initial image classification. This model has been trained on thousands of dermatoscopic images to distinguish between benign and malignant lesions.


How it works:

  • Step 1: Image preprocessing (Resizing to 224x224).
  • Step 2: Feature extraction using VGG16 layers.
  • Step 3: Classification (Confidence score calculation).
  • Step 4: Generative AI (Gemini) creates a readable medical explanation.
